This course is a comprehensive journey into the world of Graphic Design, crafted for both beginners and aspiring professionals. From visual storytelling and design principles to hands-on tools and branding essentials, the course blends creativity with industry relevance. You'll explore real-world design challenges, guided step-by-step to build a portfolio that stands out.

What You'll Gain
  • Master fundamental design principles and visual hierarchy
  • Proficiency in industry-standard design software
  • Brand identity creation and logo design expertise
  • Print and digital design portfolio development
  • Typography mastery and color theory application
Course Coverage - GD & VP
  • Graphic Design Fundamentals
  • Visual Principles & Composition
  • Brand Identity Development
  • Print Design & Production
  • Digital Design & Web Graphics
  • Portfolio Creation & Presentation
Tools Covered
  • Adobe Photoshop - Advanced photo editing and manipulation
  • Adobe Illustrator - Vector graphics and logo design
  • Adobe InDesign - Layout design and publication
  • Figma - Digital design and prototyping
  • Canva - Quick design solutions and templates
